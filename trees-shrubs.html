Blackwood - Acacia melanoxylon

Fast-growing (5-15m height) with dark green leaves. Flowers are creamy yellow appearing in late winter and early spring. Prefers moist, well-drained soils in a sheltered position. Growth is often stunted in exposed positions.

Drooping Sheoak - Allocasuarina verticillata

Small erect tree with a dense rounded crown and drooping grey-green branches. Bark fissured. Prefers well drained soils and full sun. Habitat for birds and caterpillars.

Fragrant Saltbush - Rhagodia parabolica

Low-growing, spreading shrub with grey-green leaves. Clusters of red berries in late spring and summer. Prefers well drained soils. Drought tolerant. Catterpillar friendly.

Hedge Wattle - Acacia paradoxa

A dense, thorny shrub with small dark green leaves. Large, ornamental golden-yellow ball flowers in spring. Prefers well drained soils. Excellent bird habitat. Thorns help to protect birds and other animals from predators.

Lightwood - Acacia implexa

Graceful tree (6-8m height) with an open crown. Sickle-shaped green leaves. Rough bark. Clusters of cream-yellow flowers in summer. Usually found in dry sites adjacent to waterways. Very hardy and drought tolerant. Protects understory plantings such as Correa glabra and Lonandra longifolia.

Manna Gum - Eucalyptus viminalis

Fast-growing, large tree (10-25m height) with dark green lanceolate leaves. Rough bark at base and mostly smooth bark on upper trunk and brances. Clusters of white flowers in summer and autumn. Mostly on river flats. Prefers deep, moist, well-drained soils.

River Red Gum - Eucalyptus camaldulensis

Large tree with open spreading crown. White flowers in summer. Prefers open position in deep moist soil. Tolerates inundation and dry periods once established. Foliage often affected by lerps. Bird habitat and olders trees have many hollows.

Rock Correa - Correa glabra

Dark green, oblong leaves with yellow-green tubular flowers on stems. Flowers from May to August. Can have a long flowering period up to February to September. Prefers an open sunny position in dry, well drained soils.
