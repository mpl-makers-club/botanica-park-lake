To help the planet we are Rewilding our Waterways.

This involves replanting our waterways to provide more habitat for animals, and keeping our waterways free of litter and pollution.

We need your help.

Lake History

In 2019 the water quality of the lake was so bad that there was a disease outbreak at the lake which resulted in the death of many birds and fish.

New plantings at the lake and less duck feeding has improved the lake quality.

We are also working on ways to reduce the amount of litter in the lake and around the park.
