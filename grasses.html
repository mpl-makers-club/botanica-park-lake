Frogs at Botanica Park Lake

For a long time there were no frogs at Botanica Park Lake.

Recently, a Striped March Frog has returned because there is more vegetation for habitat.

Download the Frog Census app from Melbourne Water to find out more about these frogs.

Frog friendly plant species

Water ribbons - Triglochin procera

Austral Buttercup - Ranunculus lappaceus

Rushes - Juncus spp.

Sedges - Carex spp.

Slender Knotweed - Persicaria decipiens

Spike-rushes - Eleocharis spp.

Common reed - Phragmites australis

Cumbungi - Typha domingenis

Water Milfoil - Myriophyllum crispatum
