Friends of Botanica Park Lake

In 2019 the water quality of the lake was so bad that there was an outbreak of Avian botulism which resulted in the death of many birds and fish.

New plantings at the lake and less duck feeding has improved the lake quality.

Students at the Mill Park Library Makers Club have been working on a range of technologies to help improve Botanica Park Lake.

They are currently testing a GPS Litter Picker to remove litter and using a soil moisture sensor to monitor the health of new plantings at the lake.
