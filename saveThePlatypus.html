Feeding Animals

Please do not feed birds or animals. Feeding attracts too many birds to this wetland and reduces water quality. Feeding ends up harming the birds and the animals.

Litter

Litter and rubbish harms wildlife. Please take all litter home with you.

Fishing

No fishing is permitted on this lake. Fishing can harm native animals such as turtles and birds.

Community Planting Days

Community replanting of our waterways provides more habitat for animals. Come and join us.
